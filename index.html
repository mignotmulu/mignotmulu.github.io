Why NYC?

I made the decision to go to New York for school at the end of my senior year of high school. Growing up I always knew that I wanted to go as far away from home as possible for college, though back then I thought I wanted to go to school anywhere in California. The New School and what they promoted on their website made it seem like a dream school. With being accepted to TNS and the idea of being in THE New York City, I knew I would regret passing up this opportunity, even if that meant going alone (though I do have a friend from home who goes to Pace University, but I don't see her as much as I expected to so it doesn't count haha).
